A little about me

Hello and welcome to my website! My name is Gab Savard and I'm a 21 years old Computer Science student. I am currently in the second year of my Bachelor's with CO-OP at Dalhousie University (Halifax, NS) which I am hoping to add an honour's thesis to.

Who am I?

I am an aspiring video game developer. I have been passionate about video games for as long as I can remember and have been coding them since I was 14. Lately, I have been participating in game jams (Global Game Jam being my favourite) and I have been studying game design and game development in my free time. Lately, I also have been trying to learn more about coding game physics.

Another field of study I enjoy is statistics, specifically, statistic models and A.I.. I am currently looking to start a research project in that field.

Outside of coding, I am also a musician. I have been producing my own music since 2019 and have been releasing it since then on platforms like Spotify, Apple music and YouTube. I am planning to release more music in the coming months and I can confidently say that music is my other passion.

My hobbies

My hobbies include playing video games, playing music (who could've predicted?!), reading, hiking, traveling, cooking, and learning. My favourite video games / franchises are Assassin's Creed, Cyberpunk and City Skylines.
